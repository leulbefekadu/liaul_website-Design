Objective of the assignment

The main object of this assignment is to enable us understand HTML and CSS programming languages in order to design a web site that considers a specific target group and is organized through the principles of user interface design and user experience design (UI/UX) to satisfy or address our identified target groups. We can construct the content of our respective sites based on the understanding our current and future plans for education, professional work and other interests that we would intentionally express through our respective sites targeting different parties (organization or individuals) that may be are in their immediate or otherwise environment but can support our education other goals.
